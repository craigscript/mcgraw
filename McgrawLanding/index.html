About Us

Real Estate Investing for the everyday investor

The McGraw Group LLC, is a privately held real estate investment Firm. We acquire, renovate, manage and sell investment properties, primarily in the Single Family Housing space. We begin with an institutional grade approach to risk management and we aim to provide attractive returns for our clients. our partnership with Berkshire Hathaway Home Services ™ uniquely positions us for the best opportunities in the markets we serve.

We handle everything. Really.

Who We Are

Honest and able… Established in 2005, The McGraw Group is a Milwaukee based real estate investment firm. We focus on value add opportunities in both the single and multi family space in numerous markets. Our investors value our track record for both rentals and fix and flips. While our core competency is single-family housing, we also have investments in commercial properties. Our investment philosophy is simple, and prioritized as such: Invest only in what we understand Preserve capital Deliver the highest levels of return Offer the highest levels of liquidity

All about Process

From aquisition through rehab, to tenant onboarding and management, we handle everything for you. Our primary deliverable to our investor is to preserve their capital, then provide sutiable returns for the risk that is undertaken. We'll guide you into sensible investments while providing market expertise and offering complete transparency on risk/reward ratios.
​ Aquisition
when we decide to allocate capital, we very strictly follow our process to ensure our risks are well managed and our returns have the highest of probabilities for success. Extensive analisys is executed on each neighborhood we invest in. demographics on Income, crime, home ownership and education are just a handful of examples of the data mining our research team carries out to ensure that no one will understand our markets better than us.
​ Rehabilitation
Our rehab teams have the experience and knowledge to handle various levels of complexity for whatever challenges we face during reconstruction. We throughly evaluate the condition prior to acquisiton, from the foundation and mechanicals to the roof and exterior to provide a comprehensive scope of work and to ensure the quality of work meets - if not exceeds - the standards we provide to our investors.
​ Tenant Onboarding
a credit worthy tenant is paramount and should be an objective placed well ahead of all other underwriting criteria. The quality of your investment is wholly dependant on the tenant's ability to pay their rent. Prior to application acceptance We make certain to understand our tenants' credit and background, then properly account for any additional risk.
​ Management
the foundation of our business model is to recruit and retain creditworthy tenants who properly care for your home during their residence. We have a demonstrated track record for exceeding these goals, and we accomplish this by being hyper focused on our 3 core principles:

​​ 1. Acquire homes in the best locations
2. Renovate to standards that afford our tenants luxury living
3. provide our tenants with exceptional service

​ Property Sale
A sound exit strategy is a critical component of investment success. We'll advise you in making wise investment decisions with respect to ROI as well as pricing assets in line to their mark to market.

Tenant

A tenant centric approach is at the core of our business model. Your Return on Investment is only as good as the quality of the tenant living in your home. That's why we've partnered with Berkshire Hathaway Home Services ™ to manage our homes.
We underwrite our tenants by using an institutional grade approach to managing credit risk prior to lease signing along with active management of the residents' living conditions. Each resident is re-evaluated upon lease renewal.

Our 8 Step Process

  • Stable Employment History
  • Minimum 1.5 security deposit
  • Minimum credit scores
  • Criminal Background screening
  • No evictions previous 5 yrs
  • Landlord verification
  • Existing home inspection of tenant
  • Bi annual home inspections
